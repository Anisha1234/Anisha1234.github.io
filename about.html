About Me

Hi, I am Anisha Swain

  • Web Designer
  • Web Developer
  • OpenSource contributor
  • Tech Enthusiast

People say love is a very strong feeling, but so as hatred. And love stories started with hatred are the most passionate ones. My story with programming sprung just like that. I was introduced to the world of programming in the 1st semester of my undergrad study and ultimately that was the most painful subject for me. Though I was always passionate about technical stuff but was not yet sure how to take a move forward towards my passion. My hatred for programming increased with my first failed attempt in clearing interview for college Technical Society because 80% of the questions were on programming. In the second attempt, I was prepared for my war with programming and fortunately, I won it. After getting into a very inspiring environment I started my journey with googling the technical terms pronounced by people out there and that is how I came to know about web designing.The first website that I designed was the front page of my college hostel website. I had seen people using different websites thousands of time but looking at that simple HTML page that I had tapped something in my heart. At that moment I fell in love with the work I was doing. After that, I never looked back and I continued to do Python, Javascript and practical C for Arduino programming. Eventually, I learned web development with Node JS and the journey is still proceeding with image processing, machine learning and lots of other stuff like visualization.

The best thing about work with technology is, it never feels like work. It always appears like creating an impact on the life of common people and that feeling is just magical, just like being a Supergirl. As a women tech maker, I wish to feel the same thing every day when I wake up in the morning.
